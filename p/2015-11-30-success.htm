One success#

11/30

So today was a roller coaster of emotion. I began by calling my mom for help with the rent, which embarrassed the hell out of me. I was late to work because I had to stop in the bathroom and cry it out a minute. It was rough.

But then I put in a day at work. And it got better. And this evening I got a job offer! So I’ll be once again gainfully employed (it’s the guy I talked about last [Tuesday][]) after finishing at the catering company. And have all sorts of different ways to fail! (I can’t help being jazzed.)

So there it is: bad days get better. And my day was better even before it got great. There’s the lesson in that.
